Prawn Stuffed Apples

Atleast its better than banana recipe!

Shrimp Stuffed Apples is a delicious and addictive fish preparation that can be made at home. I prepare this dish often for my weekend luncheons as it is their favorite dish. Try this Shrimp Stuffed Apples recipe for yourself!


Recipe

  • Apples 6
  • Lemon juice 15 Milliliter (1 Tablespoon)
  • Mayonnaise 45 Milliliter (3 Tablespoons)
  • Tomato paste 1 Teaspoon
  • Tabasco sauce 2 Drop
  • Dill pickles 2 , sliced
  • Stuffed olives 4 , chopped
  • Pink shrimp/Grey shrimp 180 Milliliter , cooked, shelled (3/4 Cup)
  • Parsley 1 Tablespoon , chopped

Directions

  1. Slice top off apples and remove core, seeds and pulp, taking care not to pierce skin.
  2. Slice pulp into cubes and sprinkle with lemon juice.
  3. In a bowl, combine apple cubes with stuffing ingredients.
  4. Spoon stuffing into apples a few minutes before serving.
